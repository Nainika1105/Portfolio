About Me

Web Develope | UI UX Designer

Hey! I’m Nainika, an undergrad student at VIT University, specializing in Computer Science and IoT. I’m super passionate about machine learning and UI/UX design — I love how one lets me dive deep into intelligent systems, while the other lets me channel my creative side to make tech more human-friendly. Whether it’s building models, designing interfaces, or combining both to bring an idea to life, I enjoy the challenge of blending logic with aesthetics. I’m also pretty active in sports and soft skills — I believe there’s as much to learn on the field or on a stage as there is in a classroom.
